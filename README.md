Through my internships at Critical AI and the Ministry of Defence, I’ve specialized in developing low-compute AI systems, while utilising Agile project tools, named Jira and Taiga.

At Critical AI, I rapidly built and deployed AI prototypes that automated workflows and boosted human efficiency.

At the Ministry of Defence, I worked on secure AI architectures and ML models for combat systems, ensuring seamless integration without compromising security.

On the side, I like experimenting with the math and structure behind AI.
